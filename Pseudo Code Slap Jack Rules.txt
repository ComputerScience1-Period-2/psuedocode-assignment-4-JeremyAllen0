Slap Jack
Jeremy Allen
Rules: 
1. Deal out all of the cards to all of the players evenly
2. The person sitting directly to the left of the dealers plays first
3. The objective of the game is to win all of the cards in the deck
4. Each player plays a card in the middle of the playing area
5. When a jack is played the first person to slap the jack gets the jack and all of the cards under it
6. The last person with cards is the winner
